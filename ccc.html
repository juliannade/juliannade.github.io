Century City Center

A multipage luxury real estate website

Details

Background

Century City Center is a newly built 37-story tower which provides approximately 700,000 square feet of corporate office space with supporting retail, dining and other amenities. Distinguishable on the skyline by its tapered and highly sculptured profile, it deserves a debut worthy of its presence and standing as a new iconic mark on the Los Angeles skyline.

Our San Francisco marketing team at Cushman & Wakefield was tasked with repositioning the project which included a brand development, print brochure, email campaign, and marketing website. The clients were shown a first round weebly website, but were not satisfied with the look and feel. I was then brought into design the website and develop it in HTML.

I worked on...

Design

The design process went through a few itierations. The main challege was incorporating the style of the brochure while maintaining a digital feel. The final approved design was very imagery focused to display the beautiful renderings of the building and its amenities.

Development

My main focus during development was ensuring a smooth and seamleess experience for users while including load heavy aspects such as full screen imagery and virtual tours. I imletmented lazy loading of imagery in order to speed up loading times.

My Process

Siteplan

I worked with the clients to build a list of all content, copy and imagery, they wanted included in the site.

Wireframe

I created wireframes/ rough sketchs for each page to get an idea of the look and feel of the site, in order to develop them into high fideltity mock ups for client apporval.

Mock Up

I went through a few rounds of high fiedlity mock ups for the client, making tweaks each time untill each page of the site was apporved.

Development

I developed the site in HTML using the Bootstrap framework for styling, and utilized javascript libraries for the lazy loading of images and the vitual tour hosting.
