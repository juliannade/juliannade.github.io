Background

Napa Valley Film Festival showcases an outstanding presentation of independent filmmaking, while also building a strong sense of community around the art of visual storytelling. Film screenings and events are accompanied by the world-class food and wine of the stunning Napa Valley, and features lively conversations among artists and audiences. I worked with the Napa Valley Film Festival team to design and develop a stunning website that would communicate all the glory that the film festival had to offer.
