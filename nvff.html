Napa Valley Film Festival

A multipage site wide redesign

Details

Background

The Napa Valley Film Festival is a week long film festival in Napa Valley, CA that showcases great independent films and features plent of local foods and wines. I worked with the Napa Valley Film Festival team to design and develop a stunning website that would communicate all the glory that the film festival had to offer. I worked on the festival's website to improve its user interface and to get the correct information up and organized before the festival began.

I worked on...

Design

I worked with the festival team to determine the needs for the new website. They wanted a more streamlined and consistent look, with easily accessible content. I had to work within the limitations of it being a wordpress site, so I designed for those capabilities.

Development

Leading up to the festival I pushed each redesigned page live one week at a time after the design process had been completed. As the festival got closer I updated content changes on a daily basis, such as movie timimgs or recently announced festival news.

My Process

Siteplan

Mapped out website content

Design System

Created color scheme and wireframed several page layouts

Mock Up

Created finalized designs for individual pages.

Development

With the mock up apporoved I developed the site to match the mock up, and pushed the changes live.

The Outcome

The outcome was a website with a consistent look and feel, with easily accessible information. The main changes I made were:

  • Creating consistent page structure
  • Creating a consistent color scheme
  • Making information more easly accessible through jump links, redesigined navigation, and a redeigned home page
