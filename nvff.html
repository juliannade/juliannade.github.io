About

Napa Valley Film Festival

A multipage site wide redesign

Details

Background

The Napa Valley Film Festival is a week-long film festival in Napa Valley, CA that showcases great independent films and features plenty of local foods and wines. I worked with the Napa Valley Film Festival team to design and develop a stylized website that would communicate all that the film festival had to offer.

I worked on...

Design

I worked with the festival team to determine the needs for the new website. They wanted a more streamlined and consistent look, with easily accessible content. I had to work within the limitations of it being a wordpress site, so I designed for those capabilities.

Development

Leading up to the festival I pushed each redesigned page live one week at a time after the design process had been completed. As the festival got closer I updated content changes on a daily basis, such as movie timings or recently announced festival news.

My Process

Site Plan

I met with the festival team to determine all the content, and a schedule for content to be uploaded.

Design System

I finalized the color scheme and wireframed several generic page layouts, that could apply for different page types.

Mockup

I created finalized individual designs for each page.

Development

When each mock up was approved I developed the page to match the mock up, and pushed the changes live on WordPress.

The Outcome

The outcome was a website with a consistent look and feel, with easily accessible information. The main changes I made were:

  • Creating consistent page structure
  • Creating a consistent color scheme
  • Making information more easly accessible through jump links, redesigined navigation, and a redeigned home page
